Vegan Fajitas

These vegan fajitas are a wonderful meatless version of traditional fajitas. This recipe can be prepared in advance or enjoyed right away.

Ingredients

Marinade:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • garlic salt to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste

Fajitas:

  • 2 small zucchini - julienned
  • 2 medium yellow squash - julienned
  • 1 large onion - sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper - cut into thin strips
  • 1 red bell pepper - cut into thin strips
  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • 1 (8.75oz) can whole kernal corn- drained
  • 1 (15oz) can black beans - drained
